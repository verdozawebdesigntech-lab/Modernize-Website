Payment, milestones, and timeline checklist

Typical payment schedule and milestone timeline. Timelines begin once content and approvals are provided.

  • Deposit: 50% to begin work.
  • Design approval: 40% due on sign-off of final designs.
  • Launch: 10% due on go-live.

Recommended milestone checklist

  1. Discovery & assets: gather logos, photos, and content.
  2. Design mockups: 1–3 rounds depending on plan.
  3. Development & content: build pages, integrate tools, populate content.
  4. QA & accessibility: test across devices and browsers.
  5. Launch & monitoring: DNS, backups, and post-launch checks.

Notes: timelines depend heavily on how quickly content and feedback are returned. Expedited delivery is available as a paid add‑on; ask for details.
